About

Student Expense Tracker (SET) helps students manage their expenses by logging daily spending and comparing it against personal budget limits per category.

Features

  • Log and categorise expenses
  • Donut chart and line graph for spending visualisation
  • Per-category budget progress bars
  • Timeline picker to filter by period
  • Regex-powered transaction search with highlighting
  • JSON import/export for data portability
  • Multi-currency conversion support
